Mythology encompasses the collection of traditional narratives and stories that are integral to various cultures and societies. These narratives often feature gods, goddesses, heroes, and mythical creatures, serving to explain natural phenomena, human behavior, and the origins of the world. Through the exploration of mythology, one can gain valuable insights into the values, beliefs, and historical contexts of different civilizations, fostering a deeper understanding of human experience across time and space.

 

Greek mythology is a collection of stories and legends that originated from ancient Greece, encompassing a rich tapestry of gods, goddesses, heroes, and mythical creatures. The Greeks worshipped a pantheon of deities residing on Mount Olympus.
